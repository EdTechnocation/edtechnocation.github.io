Ethical Compass

Focus assessment on the process, not just the final product.

Strategies for Academic Integrity

  • "Show Your Work": Students submit their AI prompt history.
  • Code Reviews: Students must explain and defend the AI's code.
  • Hybrid Assessments: Combine AI-assisted tasks with non-AI tasks.
  • Classroom AI Policy: Define acceptable use with students.
